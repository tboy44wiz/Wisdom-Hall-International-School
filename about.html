About Us

Who We Are...

Wisdom Hall International School started operations in the year 2006. The school was birthed from a TV programme - "Tomorrow's Leaders Today", that was aired on several TV stations in Lagos State.

Mission

The Mission of Wisdom Hall International School is the provision of excellent elementary tools for the training of boys and girls who will be able to impact positively on the development of their communities and the Larger Society.

Vision

Wisdom Hall International School is envisioned as a Citadel of Academic Excellence for the upbringing of Morally Sound and Total Child as the Tomorrow's Leader. The Vision started as a Children Programme (Tomorrow's Leaders Today) on Television which ran on Various channel in Lagos State, with the aim of repositioning the Youth or Kids of today as Tomorrow's Leadership Roles.

School Motto

Empowering Tomorrow's Leaders Today!

Our Facilities

Science Lab

The simplest way to make sure we raise literate children... is to show them that reading is a pleasurable activity, and that means finding books that they enjoy, giving them access to those books, and letting them read them.

Computer Lab

Our pupil are born into a world of technology and take to it like ducks to water. We make going to the computer lab a weekly occurrence. We are very glad to bring to your notice that we have an updated computer lab with sets of systems in it.

Art Lab

We have a multi-functional arts space where we hope our pupil feel relaxed, happy and creative. It is a family-friendly venue where small humans can play, scrabble and be free to make a mess, while their big fellows create something beautiful
