Who We Are

Wisdom Hall International School started operations in the year 2006. The school was birthed from a TV programme - "Tomorrow's Leaders Today", that was aired on several TV stations in Lagos State.

Mrs Ibukun Muse

(Proprietress)

Proprietress Speech

A very warm welcome to Wisdom Hall International School. My aim is to ensure that our Students have Access to the best possible opportunities during their Education, so that they can attain their full Potentials and are better prepared for the Future. Our work is focused on developing and Celebrating Students as individuals and not just as Learners. I believe that every Child,regardless of ability, deserves a first Class Education. Everyone has hidden potential to be uncovered and Nurtured and I strongly believe that together as a learning community, We can unlock this Potential.
At Wisdom Hall International School, our Students are at the heart of every decisions, Everything we do is about ensuring their experiences help to develop in aCaring, Supportive Environment with a strong Godly and Ethical Structure at the Centre of the School, We have high expectations and high aspirations for every of our students and our vision is EMPOWERING TOMOROW'S LEADERS TODAY! Hence; With great delight I Welcome you to our School Official Website.

Nursery

We give the best pre-basic, kindergarten training with a well structured learning space. We offer early childhood education to children before they begin compulsory education at primary school.

Primary

At our primary section, children receive primary or elementary education which prepares them to face advance learning in secondary section.
